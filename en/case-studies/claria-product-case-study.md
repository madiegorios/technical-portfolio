# Claria — Safe Product Case Study

## Overview

Claria is a private personal finance management application that helps users organize expenses, extract information from documents, and visualize financial data through dashboards.

The source code, internal architecture, database structure, prompts, business logic, and implementation details are private. This case study only describes the project at a safe, high level.

## Problem

Personal finance information is often fragmented across bank movements, invoices, receipts, notes, spreadsheets, and manual calculations. This makes it harder for users to understand spending patterns, detect recurring costs, and make informed financial decisions.

## Product goal

The goal of Claria is to simplify personal financial organization by turning scattered financial information into structured, visual, and easier-to-understand insights.

## High-level solution

Claria explores three main areas:

1. **Expense organization** — helping users classify and review personal finance movements.
2. **Document information extraction** — reducing manual work when reviewing receipts, invoices, or financial documents.
3. **Dashboards and visualization** — presenting financial data in a clearer and more actionable way.
